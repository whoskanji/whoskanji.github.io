Shadow X Documentation

Shadow X is a Snapchat tweak that aims to be among the best Snapchat tweaks, but not copy the others.

With Shadow X, you can:

- Save Media (Stories, Snaps)

- Upload Media from your camera roll

- Spoof the number of view and screenshots on your story (locally only)

- And so much more!

Disclaimer:

Shadow X is in a state of "Use at your own risk". We are not to be responsible if your account is subject to a lock or ban. This disclaimer is here to protect us.

Frequently Asked Questions

"What Snapchat version should I use with Shadow X?"

Shadow X was built for 11.80, but if you update past it (as on 5/11/22) Shadow X may experience some issues.

"I have installed Shadow, but I do not see it within Snapchat. What's wrong?"

After installing Shadow and you look in Snapchat and you don't see "Shadow" at the top of your chats/stories view, install Choicy and *only* enable the "shadow" bundle and try again.

"Some features are greyed out in Shadow X's settings. What's going on?"

If you are in Shadow X's settings and you happen to try to turn on a settings and it won't because it's greyed out, that means that we have disabled that feature server side. This could mean that the feature is broken and we have to patch it, but we don't want you to be left with a broken feature. Once we have the issue fixed, we will push an update on my repo and enable the feature again.
